10 Best Chai Lattes in the World

Chai lattes are my favorite drink. They are made of black tea with a unique spice blend and milk. It is commonly known as a chai latte, but it is originally a desi or South Asian drink called chai. I have been to coffee shops in many different states and countries and found my favorites. Here is a list of them.

North America

Starbucks

3230 E. East Sunshine St
Springfield, MO
65804
(417) 340-6294
Link to Starbucks’ website.
This is the most accessible chai throughout the United States, and it shows.

1455 W Battlefield Rd

This is a regional chain of coffee shops in southwestern Missouri, and the chai is better than Starbucks.

1221 S Main St

80501
